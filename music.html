Radiohead - Kid A

Wikipedia, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kid_A
While far from the most experimental piece of musical artistry that has ever been released, Radiohead's cult-classic Kid A is just experimental enough to be quite a trip and a sonic journey that you won't regret embarking on. There are many interpretations of the songs and even the overarching story presented, but that's a part of the fun: deciding for yourself what this bleak, electronic, absolutely mindbending project means to you.
